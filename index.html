About

I am an Associate Professor of Information Systems at Korea University Business School. My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, IT and Societal Outcomes, and Business Analytics, investigating how AI applications and digital technologies impact local economies, consumer behaviors, and business ecosystems.

Prior to joining Korea University in 2020, I served as a faculty member at Sungkyunkwan University (2017–2020) and Nanyang Technological University in Singapore (2015–2017). I received my Ph.D. in Information Systems from Arizona State University in 2015.

My work has been published in leading journals including MIS Quarterly, Information Systems Research, Journal of the Association for Information Systems, Journal of Management Information Systems, and Expert Systems with Applications. I also serve as a Director Board member at the KU ESG Research Institution and have consulted for companies including NAVER, Kakao Mobility, LG Electronics, Samsung Electronics, NAVER WebToon, Hospitals, Banks, Law Firms, Concsulting Frims and Goverment Agencies.

Research Lab

Our lab studies the intersection of data analytics, digital innovation, and online platforms. We investigate how digital technologies transform business ecosystems, from mobile app markets and e-commerce platforms to live streaming commerce and sharing economy services. We welcome motivated students who are passionate about data-driven research in information systems.
